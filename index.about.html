Abstract

The Metagascar project was born from the need for people to connect at a higher level of consciousness. The basic human need is to connect with like-minded individuals to share experiences, give advice and have fun that enriches and drives personal growth through community interaction. Metagascar is uniquely doing this by building a digital version of a community to collaborate, interact, educate, and play games. Essentially, it will be an NFciTy (Non Fungible City).

The current and the next generation are connected to the internet more than ever before. They'll be one step removed from the physical world. The metaverse will be interwoven in the fabric of their lives, from music, the arts to cross-continental communication. This new decentralized yet interconnected community of individuals will spend time online through avatars in virtual spaces on various platforms. Metagascar will be an excellent place for people to experience live music, education, mental health, drug rehab, and multiple forms of arts virtually for the first time. By enabling those who are geographically located in other parts of the country and world, they will have a safe place to interact with one another.

Introduction

At its core, Metagascar is a LOOT NFT project which is the basis of the building block for the community to take the lead. The properties are set up in a community structure with a total of 20 lots per community. Their street name identifies each community. The lot size, home size, and home styles are the characteristics listed in the LOOT NFT.

Metagascar will have a president and a vice president that the entire community will vote into office. Gastonian Punks OG NFT owners will be able to run for President. Voting is cast via Gastonian Punk OG and Gastonian Punk NFTs. Similarly, each community will have an HOA leader voted into office by their respective community.

Community

Each community will have a Homeowner Association (HOA). Property owners may elect a community board leader to represent the entire community's interest. The 20th house in each community will be reserved as a common congregation area for property owners to socialize, propose changes, and cast votes. The community member can submit proposals, i.e. change the street's name, allow holiday decorations, share resources, etc.

There are 16,000 properties in Metagascar that are split between the Ethereum and Polygon blockchains. Properties identified from #1 to #1000 are reserved for landowners minted on the Polygon blockchain. As of this writing, properties from #1001 to #8000 are available for minting on the Ethereum blockchain. Introduction

How to Develop Property

We are committed to open-source technology for flexibility and upgradability. We believe this technology will improve over time, allowing us to migrate with ease. Currently, there are several tools available to our community that help them realize their virtual dreams.

If a community member wishes to customize a property and build from scratch, they can start with Blender, Autocad and Mozilla Spoke software. Within the Mozilla Spoke environment, one can discover and explore 3D models for Sketchfab and Google Poly. Members are in complete control of their creation.

Alternatively, 3D homes are available for purchase from Sketchfab and Google Poly with a low poly count that requires minimal editing. If editing is required, import the 3D image to Blender for final touches or reduce the poly count, i.e. remove furniture, adjust scene, change color, etc. Upon completion, the 3D image can be uploaded to Sketchfab. Next, ensure that the 3D model matches the on-chain data (square-foot) of the property NFT. Upon completion, in Mozilla Spoke, publish the project to Mozilla Hubs. Then submit the Mozilla Hubs URL link to Metagascar for approval. Lastly, the Metagascar team will update the Web3 app database linking the NFT with the VR experience to your property.

There are three NFTs, LA Punks, Gastonian Punk OG, and Gastonian Punk, each having various utilities.

LA Punk holders will have roles matching real-life work experience including onboarding rights. Gastonian Punk OG holders will have the ability to cast a vote and run for President or Vice President of Metagascar. Gastonian Punk holders will be able to participate in a jury trial. LA Punk LA Punks are NFTs with specific job titles called "roles" (https://opensea.io/collection/la-punks). Roles are identical to the real-world names, such as Lawyer, Designer, Judge, Architect, Educator, etc. LA Punk NFTs will not be sold on the open marketplace and will only be distributed to community members after completion of an interview. The intent is to own an NFT that directly matches the members' actual work experience. Active and ambitious community members can potentially receive more than one LA Punks NFT giving them onboarding rights to interview others with matching roles. A holder of an LA Punk will be able to perform the specific job identified by the NFT in Metagascar. Ownership is defined as having the NFT in a community member's wallet. Gastonian Punk OG Gastonian Punks OG are NFTs available to purchase on the open marketplace (https://opensea.io/collection/gastonian-punks). Owners of these NFTs are natural-born citizens of Metagascar, called Gastonians. OG NFT holders will have the option to run for President or vice President of Metagascar. Additionally, they can participate in jury trials as a juror. Ownership is defined as having the NFT in a community member's wallet. Gastonian Punk Gastonian Punks NFT holders are considered to be naturalized citizens of Metagascar (https://opensea.io/collection/gastonian-punks-1). Citizens of Metagascar are called Gastonians. All Gasntonians can participate in Jury Trials as Jurors. As a Gastonian Punk, you have the right to vote in a Presidential primary election in Metagascar. Ownership is defined as having the NFT in a community member's wallet.

Community Disputes

On occasion, disputes and miscommunication between a property owner and community member or hired help (i.e., developer, designer, etc.)might occur. The Metagascar community as a whole will be able to participate in a jury trial. Trials are held in the common community center (courtyard) with a judge.

Casting a Vote

The community will create a voting wallet (transparent process) and post the wallet address in the community courtyard. Votes are cast by sending Gastonian Punk NFT to the specific wallet address. Two wallet addresses will be used if a dispute requires a" YES" or "No" vote. The duration to cast ballots will be similar to the real world. The respective community and presidential committee will manage the voting wallets. Upon completion of votes, the holders of the "voting wallet" may return the Gastonian NFT to the holders, place the NFT in the open marketplace to fund the community treasury, or use them to onboard new members. In short, the voting wallet creed is part of the movement. The community has the opportunity to creatively decide what to do with the NFTs accumulated in the voting wallets.
